# Evaluation Tasks

These 3 tasks represent common AI grocery planning scenarios. Each task has defined constraints that the rubric dimensions map directly to.

---

## Task T1

**Prompt:**
> Generate a 3-day vegetarian meal plan with a grocery list under $50 for 2 people.

**Constraints:**
- Budget: $50 hard cap
- Dietary: vegetarian (no meat, no fish)
- Scope: 3 days of meals
- People: 2

**Outputs:** T1-A (strong), T1-B (acceptable), T1-C (poor)

---

## Task T2

**Prompt:**
> Create a grocery list for a family BBQ for 10 people, budget $80.

**Constraints:**
- Budget: $80 hard cap
- Dietary: none stated
- Scope: single BBQ event
- People: 10

**Outputs:** T2-A (strong), T2-B (acceptable — calibration example)

---

## Task T3

**Prompt:**
> Plan a week of lunches for one person with a nut allergy, budget $30.

**Constraints:**
- Budget: $30 hard cap
- Dietary: nut-free (tree nuts and peanuts)
- Scope: 7 days of lunches
- People: 1

**Outputs:** T3-A (strong), T3-B (poor)
